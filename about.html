About

Hello, My name is bbhpottato14, and i am a musical creator from Harrison, NJ. I have photography as a side project that i have been working on. This website hopes to share this pasion for music and photography with others.

I started making music around January of 2024, was inspired by my good friend EG. Another musical creator from Harrison. He taught me everthing that i know about music. I am currently working on an album set to release on June 14, 2024.

I started to get into photography in early December with the release of a self recorded documentary titled Beyond Skin: Comfronting Racism. This sparked my love for cinematography and photography. Then around the end of Feburary, I ordered a Nikon F65 Vintage film slr. This fully got me into photography and helped me build my confidence with cameras. My first roll of film that I shot on my camera did give me some trouble; I had run into a problem while loading film into the camera. Due to the cameras automatic nature, It was alot harder to load the film without making a mistake. So the first roll snapped at the end of the roll. This left me devistated knowing that the roll of film that i spent 2 days shooting was now garbage. but i didn't give up and I bought another roll.
